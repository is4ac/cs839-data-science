 
Dominic Vose has previously played for clubs including Colchester and ScunthorpeNational League strugglers Chester have brought in former Bromley striker Dominic Vose on a short-term non-contract basis.Vose has been without a club since leaving Bromley on 1 February.The 24-year-old scored once in six National League appearances for Bromley, who he joined in December after a short spell with National League South club Whitehawk.

He could make his Chester debut against Eastleigh on Saturday.Chester are 23rd in the fifth tier, five points from safety.
