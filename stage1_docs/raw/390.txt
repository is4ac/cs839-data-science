


The visitors dominated from start to finish and broke the deadlock on 14 minutes when Ilkay Gundogan guided a header into net from Kevin De Bruyne's corner. 
Moments later, Bernadro Silva doubled their advantage with a neat finish before Sergio Aguero's long-range effort gave City a 3-0 lead going into the break. 
Gundogan bagged his second and City's fourth with a wonderful curling strike to round off a spectacular night for Pep Guardiola's side.
